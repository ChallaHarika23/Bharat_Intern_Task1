Internet of Things (IoT)

IoT refers to the interconnection of everyday objects or devices to the internet, allowing them to collect and exchange data. This technology has applications in various sectors, including smart homes, healthcare, and industrial automation.

Some popular IoT devices include smart speakers, connected thermostats, and wearable health trackers.
